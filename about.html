Kelly Askew

I am a graphic designer based in Washington, DC. I love all things infographic-related and have an affinity for clean layouts and colorful illustrations.

After growing up and attending university in North Carolina, I moved to DC and began working as a graphic designer for various international organizations, most recently as government contractor for USAID's Office of US Foreign Disaster Assistance. With a background in publication design, I have spent the last few years animating, illustrating, coding, and building upon my print foundation.

When not designing, I can be found enjoying an iced coffee at my local coffee shop, playing volleyball, biking around town, or cheering on my beloved Tar Heels.
